How to Use This Calculator

  • Trade amount: How much USD you're putting into this trade.
  • Raw spread: The price difference between the buy exchange and sell exchange, as a percentage. Find this on the live monitor.
  • Buy/sell fee: Standard trading fees per leg. Binance charges 0.10% (or 0.075% with BNB). Use presets to quickly fill common exchanges.
  • Transfer fee: The cost to move coins from the buy exchange to the sell exchange. Use $0 if you have funds pre-positioned on both exchanges.

What Is "Break-Even Spread"?

This is the minimum spread needed to cover all your fees and not lose money. At buy fee 0.10% + sell fee 0.10%, your break-even is 0.20%. Any spread above 0.20% is profit; below it is a loss.

If you also have a $2 transfer fee on a $1,000 trade, that adds 0.20% — so your break-even rises to 0.40%. This is why fast transfers (XRP, SOL) or pre-positioning capital are important for profitability.

Tips for Maximizing Profit

  • Use BNB on Binance to reduce buy fee from 0.10% to 0.075% — saves 25% on every trade.
  • Pre-position capital on both exchanges to eliminate the transfer fee entirely.
  • Choose fast coins — XRP, SOL, TRX have near-instant transfers and tiny fees.
  • Only trade spreads above 0.30% when starting — gives you comfortable margin after fees and slippage.
  • Scale capital gradually — the same % return on $10,000 earns 10× more than on $1,000.
