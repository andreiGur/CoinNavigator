How to compare exchanges (what matters)

  • Trading fee tier (maker/taker).
  • Withdrawal fee (SOL withdrawals can vary).
  • Liquidity (thin orderbooks cause slippage).
  • Deposits/withdrawals reliability (maintenance windows are common).
  • Region/KYC limits (varies widely).
