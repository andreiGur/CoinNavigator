MEXC Review (for Arbitrage)

A venue to compare prices and explore spreads. Always verify fees, withdrawals, and regional availability.
Open MEXC Account Learn Arbitrage Basics

Who MEXC is for

  • Users looking to compare spreads across additional venues.
  • Traders comfortable verifying fees and transfers.
  • People exploring altcoin pricing differences.

Arbitrage suitability

  • Best used as a comparison venue in spread monitoring.
  • Always confirm withdrawal availability for the asset.
  • Small test transfers first.

Pros

  • Additional price source for cross-exchange comparison.
  • Can show interesting micro-inefficiencies on some assets.
  • Useful for diversification of venues.

Cons / cautions

  • Fees and conditions vary by asset.
  • Transfer time can erase spreads.
  • Regional restrictions may apply.
