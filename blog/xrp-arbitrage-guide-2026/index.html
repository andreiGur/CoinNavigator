Why XRP Is One of the Best Coins for Arbitrage

Most coins have one big problem for arbitrage: transfer time. To move ETH on-chain takes 3–15 minutes. Bitcoin can take 30 minutes or more. During that time, the price gap you spotted can disappear entirely.

XRP solves this. The XRP Ledger confirms transactions in 3–5 seconds, and the on-chain transfer fee is fractions of a cent. This makes XRP uniquely suited for arbitrage — you can spot a gap, buy on Exchange A, transfer, and sell on Exchange B in under a minute.

✅ XRP arbitrage advantages: 3–5s transfer, ~$0.01 on-chain fee, top-5 coin by volume, listed on all major exchanges, consistent price gaps of 0.05%–0.30%.

XRP Transfer: The Practical Details

Unlike ETH or BTC, XRP transfers between exchanges are almost instant. Here's what you need to know:

  • Speed: 3–5 seconds on the XRP Ledger
  • Fee: ~0.000012 XRP (less than $0.01 at current prices)
  • Destination tag: Most exchanges require a destination tag when depositing XRP. Don't forget it — missing tags can result in lost funds or delays.
  • Minimum transfer: Usually 20 XRP reserve required in wallets (this is held, not spent)

⚠️ Always double-check the destination tag when sending XRP to an exchange. Each exchange provides a unique tag — sending without it or with the wrong tag can result in funds being held until support resolves it.
