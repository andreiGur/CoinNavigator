What kills arbitrage profit (the “reality checklist”)

  • Trading fees: maker/taker fees can erase small spreads.
  • Withdrawals + network fees: moving coins between venues is rarely free.
  • Slippage: the ticker price is not necessarily your fill price.
  • Latency: spreads can disappear in seconds.
  • Transfer time: deposits/withdrawals can take minutes to hours.
